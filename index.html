Just-a-Player

This project is a music player that plays local mp3 files in a fashion similar to popular music players, like Spotify. I made this as an alternative to Spotify to stream music I own without having to be connected to the internet. It has similar capabilities, like creating/managing playlists/queues, changing volume, skipping/rewinding songs, and shuffling/looping queues.
