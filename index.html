ELLE, the EndLess LEarner

This was my senior design project. I was tasked to create a minigame to add to ELLE's AnimELLE Crossing game, in the carnival section of the map. The game's purpose is to help users learn a foreign language, and my minigame does this by encouraging users to recall answers as quickly as possible to get the maximum score, in which case special animations play and the player is rewarded.

*Note: this is not a finished product, simply a proof of concept before final implementation.

Just-a-Player

This project is a music player that plays local mp3 files in a fashion similar to popular music players, like Spotify. I made this as an alternative to Spotify to stream music I own without having to be connected to the internet. It has similar capabilities, like creating/managing playlists/queues, changing volume, skipping/rewinding songs, and shuffling/looping queues.
